About Author

My name is Luka Puharic and I am a part time student at Griffith College Dublin doing Masters in Applied Digital Media.

Thinking about the main protagonist for my website I needed to build for Internet Authoring module I decided to make a website about a person that made my childhood filled with laughter.

I was in love with Charlie Chaplin's movies since I was a kid and not only that this movies would made me laugh, I was also trying do dig for a message that Charlie Chaplin was trying to point out in every movie that he created.

This site is dedicated to Charlie Chaplin. The great artist.
